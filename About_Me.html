About Me

My life story

I was born in the vietnamese city named Saigon on October 2010. I lived in a rural city 30 minutes away called Hoc Mon where I spent my childhood with my family. As a kid I loved to go on motorcycles with my family, go to playhouses, and eat food especially KFC. When I turned 6 my mom decided to move me and her to the United States leaving my family in Vietnam and joining with a small family of cousins I had in Marin City. My mom later settled in and got money and we moved to Corte Madera where I currently live and attend redwood.
